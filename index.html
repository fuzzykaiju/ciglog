About CigLog

CigLog is a cigarette craving & smoking logger. Log your daily cigarettes craved and smoked, and get analytics for your money spent and minutes of life lost.
Log immediately & honestly - CigLog transforms entries into analytics, charts, & timelines to help you spot patterns across various time ranges.

How to use:

  • Tap to log a craving with time & intensity
  • Tap to log a cigarette with time
  • Tap to see the day's timeline and add notes
  • Tap to edit or delete entries
  • Tap on skipped days for more actions
  • Use the side menu for charts, export/import, and settings
